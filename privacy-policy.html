Zynova Labs LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MyHeart mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Heart Rate Data

The App connects to Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) heart rate monitors to display your real-time heart rate. This data is:

  • Displayed on your screen in real-time
  • Stored only on your device (not transmitted to any servers)
  • Used solely to provide the heart rate monitoring functionality

Bluetooth Device Information

When you connect to a heart rate monitor, we store:

  • The device name (e.g., "Polar H10")
  • The device's Bluetooth address

This information is stored locally on your device to enable automatic reconnection to your preferred heart rate monitor.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your current heart rate
  • Reconnect to your previously paired heart rate monitor
  • Provide the core functionality of the App

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Your heart rate data and device information stay on your device and are never transmitted to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data. The App only communicates directly with your Bluetooth heart rate monitor.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete Data: Uninstalling the App removes all stored data
  • Clear Saved Device: You can disconnect and forget your saved heart rate monitor within the App
